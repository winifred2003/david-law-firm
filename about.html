David Harrison, LLB (Hons)

Solicitor & Managing Partner

USA Roots & Education

David Harrison was born and raised in Florida, South USA. He attended Stanford University (SU) Faculty of Laws, graduating with First Class Honors in 2008. During his time at SU, he was Senior Editor of the SU Law Journal and President of the Mooting Society.

Professional Journey

David began his career at a prestigious Magic Circle firm in the City of London, where he spent 8 years specializing in corporate transactions. In 2016, he joined a leading West End firm as a partner before founding Harrison & Associates in 2021.

Expertise & Specializations

With deep expertise in London's commercial landscape, David handles complex corporate transactions, M&A, joint ventures, and commercial property matters. He advises clients ranging from startups in Shoreditch to established businesses in Mayfair.

Personal Commitment

As a born-and-bred USA, David understands the unique challenges and opportunities of doing business in United State of American. He is committed to providing personalized, partner-level attention to every client, ensuring practical, commercially-focused legal advice.
